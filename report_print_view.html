Monthly Progress Report

Project: Zahrah Luxury Boutique Web App
Date: February 11, 2026

Executive Summary

This month focused on transforming the platform from a simple storefront into an intelligent, data-driven business tool. We successfully deployed critical Operational Research features to track profitability and optimize inventory, while simultaneously enhancing the customer shopping experience and stabilizing the administrative backend.

Key Features Delivered

1. Operational Intelligence (New)

  • Profitability Tracking:
    • Cost Price Management: Added the ability to input and track the Cost Price for each product in the Admin Panel.
    • Real-Time Margin Analysis: Launched a new Analytics Dashboard that calculates Gross Profit and Net Margin in real-time.
    • Profit Trends: Visualized revenue vs. cost performance over the last 7 days to identify profitable periods.
    • Business Value: You can now see exactly how much profit you are making, not just total revenue.

2. Inventory Optimization

  • Scarcity Marketing (Low Stock Alerts):
    • "Hurry! Only X Left": Products with low stock (< 5 units) now display urgent messages on the product page.
    • Visual Badges: A "Low Stock" red badge appears on product cards in the catalog to drive impulse clicks.
    • Business Value: Creates urgency for customers to buy now, increasing conversion rates and clearing slow-moving inventory.

3. User Experience (UX) Enhancements

  • Smart Recommendations:
    • "Curated Similarities" Widget: Automatically suggests related products on the detail page.
    • Inventory-Aware Sorting: The system now prioritizes in-stock items in recommendations, preventing users from clicking on sold-out goods.
  • Post-Purchase Flow:
    • Optimized the order confirmation screen to encourage account creation ("Sign Up" / "Log In") for better customer retention.
  • Category Unification:
    • Standardized product categories across the Storefront and Admin Panel for a consistent browsing experience.

4. System Stability & Deployment

  • Admin Panel Refinements:
    • Fixed critical bugs in the Orders, Customers, and Dashboard tabs to ensure accurate data reporting.
    • Resolved data display issues in the Admin tables.
  • Live Deployment:
    • Successfully deployed the full application to Firebase Hosting.
    • The platform is now live and accessible globally at https://zahrah-boutique.web.app.

Technical Improvements

  • Codebase Optimization: Improved build times and reduced bundle sizes for faster page loads.
  • Database Integrity: Implemented robust data models for Product costs and Order tracking to support future analytics features.

Next Steps (Upcoming)

  • Advanced Forecasting: Implementing algorithms to predict when stock will run out.
  • Dynamic Pricing: Tools to adjust prices based on demand rules.
  • Customer Segmentation: Grouping customers by spending habits for targeted marketing.
