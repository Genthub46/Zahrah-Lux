Introduction to the Executive Panel

The Zarhrah Executive Panel is the private control room for your luxury boutique, accessible at /admin. Every change you make here is reflected on the live customer-facing site in real time — no deployments needed.

Accessing the Panel

  • Navigate to /admin in your browser.
  • Log in with your authorized admin email and password.
  • If your email is not on the approved list, access is denied and the incident is logged.

Role-Based Permissions

Different admin accounts may have restricted access. For example, a view-only role cannot edit the Boutique Pages tab.

Session Security

⚠ Session Timeout
The panel automatically logs you out after 15 minutes of inactivity. A warning appears 1 minute before expiry, giving you the option to extend your session or log out gracefully.

Dashboard & Analytics

The Dashboard is your first screen after login. It provides a live snapshot of the business.

Dashboard Key Metrics

  • Total Revenue — Sum of all completed order values
  • Active Artifacts — Number of visible products in the catalog
  • Total Customers — Registered accounts in the system
  • Total Orders — Cumulative transactions across all time

Analytics Tab

Accessed via the Analytics sidebar item. Charts show revenue trends over time, top-selling products and customer view data.

ℹ Tip
Use the Dashboard's quick-action links to jump directly to flagged areas (e.g., out-of-stock products or pending orders).

Managing Artifacts (Products)

The term "Artifacts" is used throughout the panel to reflect the collectible, luxury nature of each item in the catalog.

Creating a New Product

1
Brand Strategy

Select a brand (e.g., Zara, Ashluxe) from the dropdown or create a new one via "Manage Brands". You can also auto-seed signature collections.

2
Core Info

Enter the product Designation (name), Selling Price (NGN), Cost Price (NGN), and Stock Level.

3
Category & Tags

Assign a category (e.g., Clothing, Accessories) and comma-separated search tags (e.g., "men, t-shirt, casual"). These drive the main site's navigation and filter system.

4
Variants

Toggle Sizing to add size options (e.g., S, M, L, XL) and Colors to add named colors with hex codes. Customers select these on the product detail page.

5
Visual Assets

Upload images from your device or paste direct image URLs. The first image becomes the primary catalog thumbnail.

6
Submit

Click "Create Product". The product instantly appears in the public catalog if Visibility is ON.

Inventory & Stockout Risk

The panel displays a dynamic risk label on each product card based on its current stock and average daily sales velocity:

> 2 Weeks ~X Days Left < 7 Days Left Sold Out Stagnant

Bulk Delete

Use the checkbox on each product card to select multiple items, then click Delete Selected to remove them in one operation. This action is logged in the Activity Log.

Order Fulfillment

The Orders tab gives you full visibility into every customer transaction.

Filtering & Searching Orders

  • Filter by Weekly / Monthly / Yearly / All Time using the date selector.
  • Use the left/right arrows to navigate between periods (e.g., Last Week, This Month).
  • Search by Order ID, Customer Name, or Email in the search bar.

Order Status Workflow

Each order has a fulfillment status you update inline via a dropdown in the order row:

Processing → Dispatched → Delivered

ℹ Customer Impact
When you change an order's status in the Admin panel, the customer can see the updated status on their Orders page and in their account. Real-time synchronization ensures they always see the latest status.

Home Layout Configuration

The Home Layout tab is your content management system for the main landing page. Changes update the live site instantly.

ℹ Real-Time Effect
Every toggle or product selection in this tab immediately reflects on the public homepage — no save button needed.

Global Setting: Catalog Visibility

The master switch at the top of this tab completely hides or shows the full product catalog grid at the bottom of the home page. Use this during store maintenance or before a major collection launch.

Configurable Home Page Sections

Hero Banner — The full-screen introductory image. Toggle it on/off or paste a new image URL to change the background photo.

Signature Features — The three brand pillars section (Bespoke Tailoring, Global Concierge, Authentic Luxury). Toggle to show or hide.

Boutique Banner — A mid-page editorial banner. Update the image URL and the text title displayed over it.

Lifestyle Showcase — Two side-by-side editorial mood images. Click "Edit Muses" to upload new photos or paste URLs for the Left (Accessories) and Right (Streetwear) slots.

Manor Collection — A premium horizontal product scroller. Click "Select Products" to handpick which products appear here.

Styling Ideas — The curated lookbook. Create "Looks" by uploading a styled outfit photo, then linking the individual products that make up that outfit.

Bundles & Deals — A promotional section. Handpick products to feature in this deals scroller.

Curated Categories — The tabbed product showcase. Rename tabs, hide unused ones, and select specific products for each category tab.

Custom Sections — Create entirely new carousel sections. Enter a title (e.g., "SUMMER EDIT") and click "Deploy Section", then select products.

Other Administrative Features

Customers Tab

View a full roster of registered customers — their names, emails, total spend, and order count. Useful for identifying VIP clients and understanding your customer base.

Waitlist Tab (Restocks)

When a product is out of stock, customers can submit a restock request. This tab aggregates those requests, helping you prioritize which items to replenish based on real demand.

Pricing AI Tab

A streamlined interface to adjust prices quickly across the catalog. Update selling prices without opening individual product forms, and view price change impact at a glance.

Activity Log

An immutable audit trail of all critical admin actions — including who created or deleted products, who updated layout settings, and who changed database state. Every action is timestamped and attributed to the acting admin's email.

Database Tools (in Sidebar)

Sidebar Danger Zone

  • Repair Database — Reseeds products, layout, and footer pages to the initial defaults. Does NOT delete customer accounts or orders.
  • Wipe Database — Permanently deletes ALL data including products, orders, customers, pages, and layout. Only admin login credentials are preserved.
⚠ CRITICAL WARNING: The Wipe Database action is irreversible. Always confirm you have a data backup before proceeding. This action is logged in the Activity Log.
