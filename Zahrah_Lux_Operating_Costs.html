Zahrah Luxury Collections: Infrastructure & Operating Costs

There are absolutely no hidden fees tied to the code or the app itself—you own this platform 100%, and there are no monthly subscription fees for the custom software built.

However, because this is a live web application, there are standard third-party infrastructure costs that you should be aware of as your store grows. Below is a complete breakdown of what to expect in the long run.

1. Firebase (Hosting, Database, Authentication)

  • Current Status: You are currently on the Spark (Free) plan, which handles a standard amount of traffic for free.
  • The Long Run: To enable automated emails (like order confirmations and welcome emails), you will need to upgrade to the Blaze (Pay-as-you-go) plan.
  • Cost: The Blaze plan still includes massive free tier limits. You are only charged if you exceed those limits (e.g., more than 50,000 database reads per day, or more than 10GB of hosting bandwidth per month). For a standard boutique, your bill will realistically remain at $0 or just a few cents a month unless you get massive surges in traffic.

2. Cloudinary (Admin Image Uploads)

  • Current Status: Free tier.
  • Cost: Cloudinary gives you 25 credits per month for free (roughly equating to 25,000 image views or 25GB of bandwidth). Unless you are uploading thousands of incredibly heavy images and getting thousands of daily visitors, you will likely never need to pay for this. If you eventually outgrow it, paid plans start around $99/month.

3. Paystack (Payment Gateway)

  • Cost: Paystack does not charge any monthly or setup fees. They only make money when you make money, taking a small standard percentage of each successful transaction (e.g., 1.5% + ₦100 for local Nigerian transactions).

4. Custom Domain Name

  • Cost: Your custom domain (e.g., zahrahluxury.com) will need to be renewed yearly with your domain registrar (like Namecheap or GoDaddy). This is standard for all websites and usually costs around $10 to $20 per year.

Summary

If you get 100 to 1,000 visitors a month, your monthly running cost for the entire application will realistically be $0. Your only real long-term cost is the yearly renewal of your domain name and the standard Paystack transaction cuts.

The platform is designed to scale cheaply and only cost you money when you are generating substantial revenue!
