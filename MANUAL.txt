ZARHRAH LUXURY - CLIENT MANUAL

HOW TO ACCESS THE ADMIN PANEL
-----------------------------
1. Go to the website URL.
2. Append `?admin=true` to the URL (e.g., `zahrah-boutique.web.app/?admin=true`).
3. Enter the secret passcode when prompted.

(Note: Ensure you keep the passcode secure.)

MANAGING PRODUCTS
-----------------
1. Once logged in as Admin, you will see the "Products" tab.
2. **Add Product**: Fill in the form at the top (Name, Brand, Price, etc.).
   - Images: Upload image via the "Add Image" button.
   - Variations: Add colors and sizes.
   - Click "Create Product".
3. **Edit Product**: Find the product in the list and click "Edit".
4. **Delete Product**: Click the Trash icon next to a product to remove it.

MANAGING ORDERS
---------------
1. Switch to the "Orders" tab.
2. You will see a list of all customer orders.
3. Click "View Details" to see shipping address and items.
4. Update Status: You can change status from "Pending" to "Shipped" or "Delivered".

VIEWING ANALYTICS
-----------------
1. Switch to the "Analytics" tab.
2. View "Total Period Yield" to see total revenue.
3. See "Most Viewed Products" to understand customer interest.

NEWSLETTER
----------
1. Customer emails captured via the footer "Join the inner circle" form are saved to the database.
2. Ask your developer to export this list for your marketing campaigns.

CUSTOMER WISHLIST & CART
------------------------
- Customers can save items to Wishlist (Heart icon).
- Cart items are saved even if they leave and come back.
- When they log in, their Guest Cart merges with their Account Cart.
