HOW TO ACCESS THE ADMIN PANEL
-----------------------------
1. Go to the website URL.
2. Append `?admin=true` to the URL (e.g., `zahrah-boutique.web.app/?admin=true`).
3. Enter the secret passcode when prompted.
(Note: Ensure you keep the passcode secure. Only share with authorized staff.)

THE ADMIN DASHBOARD & ITS USES
---
The Admin Panel is divided into several sections (tabs) that allow you to manage every aspect of your online boutique.

1. DASHBOARD (ANALYTICS & OVERVIEW)
-----------------------------------
- Serves as the central hub for your business metrics.
- View "Total Period Yield" to track your total revenue.
- Monitor "Most Viewed Products" and "Recent Activity" to understand customer interest and behavior.
- Use this daily to get a quick snapshot of store performance.

2. PRODUCTS TAB
---------------
- Manage your entire inventory here.
- **Add Product**: Fill in the form (Name, Brand, Price, etc.). Upload images via the "Add Image" button. Add variations (colors, sizes) and set stock levels.
- **Edit/Delete**: Find products in the list to update their details or remove them from the store completely.
- **Visibility**: You can temporarily hide products without deleting them.

3. ORDERS TAB (CUSTOMER PLACEMENTS & CHECKOUT)
----------------------------------------------
- **Checkout Process**: When a customer places an order on the storefront, the web app securely captures their shipping details and order items, saving the "placement" directly to your database.
- Track and manage all these customer purchases here.
- Click "View Details" on any order to see the shipping address, items ordered, and customer notes.
- **Update Status**: Change the status from "Pending" to "Shipped" or "Delivered".
- *Note on Order Emails*: Depending on your exact payment gateway setup, order confirmation emails are typically handled automatically by the backend or the payment provider (e.g., Paystack/Flutterwave).

4. CUSTOMERS TAB
----------------
- View a directory of all users who have created an account or placed an order.
- Track customer purchase history and total spend.
- Useful for identifying VIP clients for special outreach.

5. LAYOUT TAB (MANAGING HOME PAGE SECTIONS)
-------------------------------------------
This is where you control the visual appearance and curated collections on your storefront. The Home Page is built using modular sections:

- **Boutique Banner**: Update the large hero image and text at the top of the homepage.
- **Manor Collection / Styling Ideas**: Select specific, high-end pieces to feature prominently. You can hand-pick the exact products that appear here.
- **Bundles Deals / Last Chance To Buy**: 
  * Used for clearance sales, special promotions, or grouped item discounts.
  * You MUST explicitly select products for this section to make it appear.
  * If no products are selected, the entire "Bundles Deals" section automatically hides itself to keep the homepage clean.
- **Lifestyle Showcase**: Update the atmospheric/editorial images that give your brand its luxury feel.
- **Toggle Visibility**: Every section has a toggle switch. If you want to temporarily hide the "Styling Ideas" section, just toggle it off.

6. PAGES TAB
------------
- Manage textual content across the site.
- Create or edit footer pages like "About Us", "Shipping Policy", "Terms of Service", etc.

7. REQUESTS & ACTIVITY LOGS (WAITLISTS & COMMUNICATIONS)
--------------------------------------------------------
- **Waitlist Intelligence (Restock Requests)**: 
  * If a product is out of stock, customers can click to join a priority "Waitlist" on the storefront.
  * The web app captures their Name, Email, or WhatsApp number and saves it here.
  * *Sending Mail & Notifications*: In this tab, you will see a list of everyone waiting for specific items. Next to their name are quick-action buttons. 
  * Clicking the **Email icon** automatically opens your default email app (like Gmail or Outlook) with a pre-written message ("Good news! The item is back in stock...").
  * Clicking the **WhatsApp icon** opens WhatsApp Web/App with a pre-written message to send directly to their verified Nigerian phone number.
  * Once notified, you can change their status from "Pending" to "Notified" to keep track.
  * You can also click "Export Report" to generate a PDF of your waitlist for inventory planning.
- **Client Feedback (Reviews)**: View and manage customer reviews.
- **Activity Log**: An audit trail showing what actions were taken in the admin panel (e.g., who changed a price, who deleted a product). Good for accountability.

8. PRICING TAB
--------------
- Allows for bulk adjustments or advanced pricing strategies across your catalog without editing products one by one.

GENERAL STOREFRONT FEATURES
---

NEWSLETTER & MARKETING
----------------------
1. Customer emails captured via the "Join the inner circle" form on the storefront are automatically saved to your secure database.
2. **Exporting your list**: You no longer need a developer to export this list! Simply go to the **Customers Tab** in your Admin Panel and click the "Export Newsletter List" button at the top right to download a CSV file ready for Mailchimp or your preferred marketing tool.

CUSTOMER WISHLIST & CART ABANDONMENT
------------------------------------
- **Wishlist**: Customers can save items to their personal Wishlist (Heart icon) to review or purchase later. 
- **Persistent Cart**: Items added to the cart are saved securely. If a customer leaves the website and returns days later, their cart will be waiting for them.
- **Account Syncing**: When a guest user creates an account or logs in, their temporary cart merges seamlessly with their main account cart, preventing data loss.
